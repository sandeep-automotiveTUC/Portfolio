About Me

I'm an R&D Software Developer & Test Engineer with extensive experience in autonomous driving, ADAS systems, and automotive software development. Currently working at Momenta GmbH, I specialize in perception algorithms, object detection, and multi-modal sensor fusion for L2/L2++ autonomous driving systems.

My expertise spans from developing ML models for CPU-usage prediction to architecting virtual test environments for E-Mobility software validation. I hold an M.Sc. in Automotive Software Engineering from Technical University of Chemnitz (Grade: 1.8) and have worked with leading automotive companies including BMW AG and CARIAD SE.

I'm passionate about robotics, embedded systems, CAN/CAN-FD protocols, and ROS/ROS2 frameworks. My work involves end-to-end development from algorithm design to system validation, following ISO 26262 standards and V-Model development lifecycle.
